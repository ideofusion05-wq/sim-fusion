The problem

Real training carries real risk, and real cost.

Institutions are forced to choose between realism and access. SIMFUSION removes that trade-off.

Expensive

Hardware-locked systems

Premium simulation hardware can cost tens of thousands of dollars per unit — out of reach for most institutions.

Limited

Single-purpose platforms

Most tools serve one domain, one device, and rarely scale beyond a single classroom or lab.

Risky

No room to practice

Some scenarios are too costly, too rare, or too dangerous to rehearse in the real world at all.
